Did you know? Penguins mate for life! They find their mate by presenting them with a pebble as a symbol of their love.

Join our Penguin in an adventure to find it's lifelong mate

Game Instructions

Controls:
Use the up arrow key to make the penguin jump. Avoid colliding with the evil snowman, as it will cost you a life.
Objective: Collect hearts scattered throughout the game to fill the heart bar and acquire the diamond. You have 3 lives to complete your mission. Be careful not to lose them all!

Lives: 3
